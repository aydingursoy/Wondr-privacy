Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Wondr ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number (if using phone authentication)

Trip Data

When you use our trip planning features, we collect:

  • Travel preferences (destination, dates, budget, lodging style)
  • Generated trip itineraries
  • Personal notes you add to activities

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage statistics (features used, trips generated)
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized travel itineraries
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important service notifications
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • iOS Keychain for sensitive credentials

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Database and authentication
  • OpenAI - AI-powered trip generation (no personal data shared)
  • Apple - In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Affiliate Partners - Booking links (Booking.com, Viator, etc.)

Affiliate Disclosure

Wondr earns commissions when you book through our affiliate links. This does not affect the price you pay. We clearly disclose affiliate links within the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your trip data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Wondr is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
