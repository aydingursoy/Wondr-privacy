Frequently Asked Questions

How does Wondr generate trips?

Wondr uses AI to create personalized travel itineraries based on your preferences, budget, and travel style. Each trip is tailored specifically to you.

Is the trip planning really free?

Yes! You can generate and view complete trip itineraries for free. We earn revenue through affiliate partnerships when you book accommodations or activities through our links.

What's included in Wondr Pro?

Wondr Pro gives you unlimited trip generation, more rerolls per trip, priority support, and access to exclusive features as we add them.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Wondr.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any in-app purchase.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data.

Are the prices accurate?

We provide estimated costs based on typical prices for your chosen lodging style and destination. Actual prices may vary based on season and availability.

Do you sell my data?

No. We never sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details on how we protect your information.
